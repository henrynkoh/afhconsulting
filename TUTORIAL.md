# AFH Success Consultant Curriculum - Step-by-Step Tutorial

## Welcome to Your Learning Journey

This tutorial will guide you through the AFH Success Consultant Curriculum step-by-step. Follow along to maximize your learning experience.

## Tutorial Overview

**Estimated Time**: 15-20 hours total
**Structure**: 6 modules + resources + exam
**Format**: Self-paced, interactive web application

## Getting Started (30 minutes)

### Step 1: Access the Application

1. **Start the development server** (if running locally):
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   ```

2. **Open your browser** and navigate to:
   ```
   http://localhost:3000
   ```

3. **Familiarize yourself with the homepage**:
   - Review the 6 module cards
   - Note the additional resources section
   - Understand the navigation structure

### Step 2: Set Up Your Learning Environment

1. **Prepare note-taking tools**:
   - Physical notebook or digital note app
   - Highlighter for key concepts
   - Calculator for financial exercises

2. **Create a study schedule**:
   - Module 1: Day 1 (2-3 hours)
   - Module 2: Day 2 (2-3 hours)
   - Module 3: Day 3 (2-3 hours)
   - Module 4: Day 4 (2-3 hours)
   - Module 5: Day 5 (2-3 hours)
   - Module 6: Day 6 (2-3 hours)
   - Resources & Exam: Day 7 (2-3 hours)

3. **Set learning goals**:
   - What do you want to achieve?
   - What's your current knowledge level?
   - What's your timeline?

## Module-by-Module Tutorial

### Tutorial 1: Module 1 - Business Foundation (2-3 hours)

#### Before You Start
- Have a calculator ready
- Prepare to take notes on financial concepts
- Review any existing AFH business knowledge

#### Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1: Read the Module Goal** (5 minutes)
- Understand what you'll learn
- Note the key concepts highlighted

**Step 2: Study Financial Reality Check** (30 minutes)
- Read the Medicaid vs. Private Pay comparison table
- Calculate the difference: $4,000 vs. $8,500 resident
- Note the profit margin differences
- **Action**: Write down 3 key takeaways

**Step 3: Understand Goal Setting** (30 minutes)
- Review the Owner's Draw calculation
- **Exercise**: Calculate your own target:
  - Desired annual income: $_______
  - Monthly target: $_______ ÷ 12 = $_______
- **Action**: Document your financial goals

**Step 4: Study the Case Study** (20 minutes)
- Compare the two resident scenarios
- Calculate net profit for each
- **Action**: Create your own comparison for your market

**Step 5: Review Scalability Mindset** (30 minutes)
- Read the comparison table
- **Exercise**: Rate yourself:
  - Are you more Caregiver-Owner or Business-Administrator?
  - What changes do you need to make?
- **Action**: Create a transition plan

**Step 6: Review Key Takeaways** (15 minutes)
- Read all takeaways
- **Action**: Write a summary paragraph

**Step 7: Move to Next Module** (5 minutes)
- Click "Next Module" button
- Or return to homepage to review

#### Module 1 Checklist
- [ ] Understood profit margin differences
- [ ] Calculated personal financial goals
- [ ] Completed case study analysis
- [ ] Identified mindset transition needs
- [ ] Documented key takeaways

### Tutorial 2: Module 2 - Site Selection (2-3 hours)

#### Before You Start
- Have access to real estate websites (Zillow, Redfin)
- Prepare to research local markets
- Have a map tool ready

#### Step-by-Step Process

**Step 1: Understand the 5-Mile Rule** (20 minutes)
- Read the explanation
- **Exercise**: Identify your target market area
  - Where do high-wealth families live?
  - What's within 5 miles?
- **Action**: Mark your target area on a map

**Step 2: Create Competitor Grid** (45 minutes)
- Use the Market Analysis Template (link provided)
- **Exercise**: Research 5 nearby AFHs
  - Find their locations
  - Estimate their rates (call if needed)
  - Note their specialties
- **Action**: Fill out the competitor grid

**Step 3: Complete Affluence Index** (30 minutes)
- Research your target area:
  - Median home value (Zillow/Redfin)
  - Nearby grocery stores
  - Hospital proximity
  - Saturation level
- **Action**: Complete the Affluence Index table

**Step 4: Identify Market Gaps** (30 minutes)
- Review the "Gap in the Market" strategy table
- **Exercise**: Based on your competitor research:
  - What gaps exist?
  - What strategy fits?
  - What pricing should you use?
- **Action**: Document your strategy

**Step 5: Practice Secret Shopper Method** (20 minutes)
- Review the script
- **Exercise**: Call 2-3 competitors (optional practice)
- **Action**: Refine your secret shopper approach

**Step 6: Review High-Acuity Opportunities** (20 minutes)
- Understand hospice-ready criteria
- **Exercise**: Evaluate a property you know:
  - Does it meet criteria?
  - What modifications needed?
- **Action**: Create property evaluation checklist

#### Module 2 Checklist
- [ ] Identified target market area
- [ ] Completed competitor grid
- [ ] Researched affluence index
- [ ] Identified market gaps
- [ ] Understood high-acuity opportunities
