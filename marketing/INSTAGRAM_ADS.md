## Instagram Ad Specifications

### Feed Ads
- **Image**: 1080 x 1080px (1:1) or 1080 x 566px (1.91:1)
- **Video**: MP4 or MOV, max 240MB, 60 seconds max
- **Caption**: Up to 2,200 characters
- **CTA**: Learn More, Shop Now, Sign Up

### Stories Ads
- **Image**: 1080 x 1920px (9:16)
- **Video**: 1080 x 1920px, 15 seconds max
- **Text**: Keep under 20% of image area

### Reels Ads
- **Video**: 1080 x 1920px (9:16), 15-90 seconds
- **Audio**: Original or licensed music
- **Text Overlays**: Recommended for engagement

## Engagement Strategy

### Posting Schedule
- **Feed Posts**: 3-5 times per week
- **Stories**: Daily
- **Reels**: 2-3 times per week

### Best Times to Post
- Monday-Friday: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM
- Weekends: 10 AM-12 PM

### Engagement Tactics
- Respond to comments within 2 hours
- Use Instagram Stories polls and questions
- Host Instagram Live sessions
- Collaborate with influencers in healthcare/senior care
- Share user-generated content

## Performance Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track
- **Reach**: Number of unique accounts
- **Impressions**: Total number of times ad shown
- **Engagement Rate**: (Likes + Comments + Saves) / Impressions
- **Click-Through Rate**: Clicks / Impressions
- **Cost Per Click**: Ad spend / Clicks
- **Conversion Rate**: Conversions / Clicks

### Target Benchmarks
- **Engagement Rate**: 3-6%
- **CTR**: 1-3%
- **CPC**: $0.50-$2.00
- **Conversion Rate**: 2-5%
