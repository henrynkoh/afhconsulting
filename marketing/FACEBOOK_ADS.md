## Campaign Settings

### Budget Recommendations
- **Awareness**: $50-100/day
- **Conversion**: $100-200/day
- **Retargeting**: $25-50/day

### Ad Placements
- Facebook Feed
- Instagram Feed
- Instagram Stories
- Facebook Stories
- Messenger (optional)

### Optimization
- **Awareness**: Optimize for Link Clicks
- **Conversion**: Optimize for Conversions (landing page views)
- **Retargeting**: Optimize for Conversions

### Tracking
- Facebook Pixel installed on landing page
- Conversion events: PageView, Lead, Purchase
- UTM parameters for tracking

---

## A/B Testing Ideas

1. **Headlines**: Problem-focused vs. Solution-focused
2. **Images**: Professional photos vs. Infographics
3. **CTAs**: "Learn More" vs. "Get Started" vs. "Enroll Now"
4. **Text Length**: Short (125 chars) vs. Long (500 chars)
5. **Video vs. Image**: Test video ads vs. static images

---

## Compliance Notes

- Include disclaimer: "Results may vary. Individual results depend on market conditions and implementation."
- Ensure all claims are truthful and verifiable
- Follow Facebook advertising policies
- Include privacy policy link
- Comply with healthcare advertising regulations if applicable

---

## Performance Metrics to Track

- **CTR** (Click-Through Rate): Target 2-5%
- **CPC** (Cost Per Click): Target $0.50-$2.00
- **CPM** (Cost Per Mille): Target $5-$15
- **Conversion Rate**: Target 3-7%
- **CPA** (Cost Per Acquisition): Target $20-$50
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend): Target 3:1 or higher
