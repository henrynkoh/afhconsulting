## Email Best Practices

### Design
- Mobile-responsive (test on phones)
- Clean, professional layout
- Clear hierarchy
- Readable fonts (16px minimum)
- High contrast colors

### Content
- Personalize with [First Name]
- Clear subject lines (50 characters max)
- Single, clear CTA
- Scannable format
- Value-focused

### Timing
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM
- Avoid: Mondays, Fridays, weekends
- Consider time zones

### Testing
- Test on multiple email clients
- Check spam score
- Verify all links
- Test mobile view
- Proofread carefully

### Metrics
- Open rate (target: 20-25%)
- Click-through rate (target: 3-5%)
- Conversion rate (target: 1-3%)
- Unsubscribe rate (target: <0.5%)
